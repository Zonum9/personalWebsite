About me

Thank you for taking a look at my website! My name is Andres Munoz, a motivated X year old developer eager to get into the professional field.

Most of my experience comes from school projects, however I always made sure to go above and beyond to ensure that confidently say that I understand what I am doing, and can apply it in the real world. I am currently enrolled in McGill university and expect to finish my studies in April 2025.

On a personal level, I am a very curious person, always looking for new and interesting topics to learn about. I also enjoy playing videogames and spending time with my family.

Chess-game GUI

Made a fully functional GUI for a chess game using JavaFX. This was my final project for college, and it was made in collaboration with 2 other people, who were handling chess engine logic and local online play respectively. All menu logic and controls were implemented by me. I also helped with merging all the individual components together, debugging them and adding additional functionalities to them when necessary.
